Pasta with Sauce

It's like pasta but it has sauce on it.

Pasta with Sauce

Ingredients

  • 500g of pasta
  • 10L of water
  • A handful of salt
  • 1 jar of pasta sauce

Method

  1. Put a handful of salt into the water and bring the water to the boil in a large pot.
  2. Once the water is boiling, stir it then put the whole pack of pasta into the water.
  3. Cook the pasta for about 10 minutes or however long it says on the pack.
  4. Once it is cooked strain the pasta and put the pot back on the heat.
  5. Empty the jar of sauce into the pot and cook on a medium heat until it is hot.
  6. Mix the pasta into the sauce then eat it, probably from a bowl but that's not completely necessary.
