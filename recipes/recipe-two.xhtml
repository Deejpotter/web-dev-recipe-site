Plain Rice

Plain rice made the absorbtion way.

Plain Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • A pinch of salt

Method

  1. Wash the rice twice in cold water then strain and empty into a pot.
  2. Tip the water into the same pot and add the salt.
  3. Cook the rice over a medium heat for about 10 minutes.
  4. Once the water is almost completely absorbed, put the lid on and reduce the heat to low for about a minute then turn the heat off. Listen for a crackling sound to know when to put the lid on.
  5. Leave the rice with the lid on and the heat off for about 10 minutes then it will be done. Don't take the lid off during this step.
